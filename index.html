About Me

I’m Allen Zagorodnyuk, a recent New York University graduate with a BA in Mathematics and minors in Economic Theory and Computer Science. I am driven by curiosity and a love of learning. I am passionate about trading and enjoy diving into data to spot patterns, run simulations, and build trading strategies. I like working on projects that push me to think creatively and solve real world financial problems. Right now, I’m eager to break into the quantitative finance field. To keep growing, I plan to pursue a Master’s in Mathematical Finance, where I can deepen my understanding of the theory and technicals that drive the investment world.

Outside my professional and academic career, I enjoy following sports, reading, and listening to podcasts. I am also very passionate about Chess, as I grew up having competed in tournaments across New York. I use chess as a way to clear my head, but also challenge myself against tough opponents.
