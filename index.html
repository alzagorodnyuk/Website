About Me

I’m Allen Zagorodnyuk, a recent graduate of New York University with a Bachelor’s in Mathematics and minors in Economic Theory and Computer Science. I’m currently pursuing entry-level and internship opportunities on the buy-side, ideally working on quantitative research and strategy development.

My interests in mathematics and financial markets date back to growing up in New York and years of competitive chess. Living in NYC sparked my curiosity about capital markets, while chess built my logical thinking and problem-solving foundation.

I’m planning to pursue a master’s in Mathematical Finance or Financial Engineering to deepen my technical understanding and accelerate my transition into quantitative finance. I’m proficient in Python and Excel, and I enjoy creating/testing trading projects and conducting market research.

Outside of my academic and professional life, I often read, listen to podcasts, follow hockey, and play chess online. Feel free to explore my Projects & Research and Experiences pages to learn more about myself and my past experiences.
