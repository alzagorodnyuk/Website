Index Rebalancing Algo Trading

Index Rebalancing Announcements are scheduled to occur every three months, so why not take opportunity of this event? Gathering historical Stocks and ETF data, I used Python and Excel to backtested algorithmic trading strategies through a modeled portfolio with $5 million in capital. I tested different buy and sell triggers and convertated my results into an clear and professional Investment Summary.
